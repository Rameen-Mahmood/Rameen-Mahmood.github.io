I'm a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at New York University (Tandon). I am part of mLab, where I'm advised by Professor Danny Huang. Prior to this, I earned my BSc in Electrical Engineering (Honors) at New York University Abu Dhabi.

My research lies at the intersection of computer networks and machine learning, with a focus on clinical applications. I explore ways to use machine learning on network traffic data to gain insights into patient well-being and cognitive states. My work aims to leverage network traffic analysis as a non-invasive tool for inferring health markers, potentially contributing to the understanding of mental health, neurodegenerative diseases, and other clinical indicators.
